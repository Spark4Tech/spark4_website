Stash It Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2025

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Our extension is designed with your data security as a top priority.

2. No Data Collection

Stash It does not collect, store, or track any of your personal data or browsing information. We respect your privacy completely.

3. How Stash It Works

  • The extension only creates calendar events in your Google Calendar
  • We never read, modify, or delete any of your existing documents or calendar events
  • The only information used is what you explicitly provide when creating a calendar event

4. Google Calendar Integration

Stash It requires Google Calendar access to create events. This access is limited to creating new calendar events

5. Permissions

The extension requests only the minimum permissions necessary to function:

  • Access to the current browser tab to create an event link
  • Google Calendar API access for event creation

6. Data Security

While the extension interacts with Google's services, we do not store or retain any of your personal information. All event creation happens directly through Google's secure APIs.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify you of any significant changes through the Chrome Web Store listing or via the extension.
