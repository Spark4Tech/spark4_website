Privacy Policy

At Spark4, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the information you share with us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of personal data:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, and other contact details you provide when signing up for our services or contacting us.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the Spark4 website and our Apps, including device information, log files, and cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your personal data is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve Our Services: We use your data to personalize your experience, improve app functionality, and provide recommendations based on your preferences.
  • Communication: We may use your email to send updates, respond to inquiries, and provide information about new features.
  • Compliance and Security: We use your data to comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, and ensure the security of our services.

3. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct or update any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request that we delete your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Data Portability: You can request to receive your data in a commonly used format and transmit it to another controller.
  • Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit the processing of your data under certain conditions.
  • Withdraw Consent: If we process your data based on your consent, you can withdraw your consent at any time.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Spark4 Apps and providing our services. These providers are bound by strict data protection agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests from public authorities.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website and app. You can control your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your personal data may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
